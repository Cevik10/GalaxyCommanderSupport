Before you write

Include this information so we can help quickly:

Common topics

  • Purchases & subscriptions: Restore purchases from the app’s store or subscription screen. If something is missing, email us with your Apple ID purchase receipt snippet (no passwords).

  • Account / progress: Explain if data is local-only or synced (adjust to match your app).
